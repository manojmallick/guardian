# Compliance Narrator — AI Decision Audit Trail Generator

> Prove to your auditor that AI-handled incidents are more transparent than manual ones.

A standalone, forkable module from the [Guardian](https://github.com/manojmallick/guardian) project — Airia AI Agents Hackathon 2026.

---

## What It Does

When an incident is resolved, generates a fully auditable post-mortem PDF containing every AI decision (model used, input data, reasoning, confidence score), every human approval (identity, timestamp), and a regulatory compliance record aligned to your framework.

## Regulatory Frameworks Supported

| Framework | File | For |
|-----------|------|-----|
| DORA Article 11 | `frameworks/dora.js` | EU financial services |
| SOX Section 404 | `frameworks/sox.js` | US public companies |
| HIPAA | `frameworks/hipaa.js` | Healthcare |
| FISMA | `frameworks/fisma.js` | US Government |

## Fork For

- **Insurance:** Claims processing AI decision audit trails
